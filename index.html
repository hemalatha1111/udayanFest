The Arenas

Six grounds. One fest.

Pick your arena, or run all six. Every event is built and judged by people who live in that world.

Build

Hackathon

24 hours, one problem statement, and a team of four. Ship a working prototype before sunrise.

Compete

Coding Contest

Timed algorithmic rounds, live leaderboard, and a final boss problem that separates the podium from the rest.

Perform

Dance Battle

Solo and crew rounds across freestyle and choreography. Judged on the main stage, in front of the whole fest.

Play

Gaming Arena

LAN tournaments across your favourite titles, bracket-style, with a big screen for the finals.

Pitch

Startup Pitch

Eight minutes to pitch, seven to defend it. A panel of founders and investors decides who's fundable.

Headline

Battle of Bands

Campus bands go head to head for the closing-night slot, right before the fest's headline act.
